1. Finance Summary Dashboard – MS Excel

An interactive Excel-based dashboard designed to track revenue, expenses, and profit performance across departments, categories, and time periods.

2. Short Description:

This dashboard provides a summarized view of an organization’s financial performance by analyzing revenue, expenses, and profit trends. It helps management monitor costs, identify profitable areas, and make better budgeting decisions.

3. Tech Stack:

* Microsoft Excel

* Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts

* Data Cleaning

* Excel Formulas (SUMIFS, IF, VLOOKUP/XLOOKUP)

* Slicers & Timelines

4. Features:

Business Problem-

Organizations often manage financial data in multiple sheets, making it difficult to:

* Track overall profit and loss

* Monitor department-wise spending

* Compare revenue against expenses

* Identify cost-heavy categories

* Manual analysis is time-consuming and prone to errors.

Goal of the Dashboard-

The main goal was to build a centralized financial monitoring system in Excel that:

* Shows key financial KPIs at a glance

* Compares revenue and expenses by department

* Tracks monthly profit trends

* Supports budgeting and cost control

Walkthrough of Key Visuals -

KPI Summary Cards (Top Section)

* Total Revenue

* Total Expense

* Net Profit/Loss

* Maximum Revenue

* Maximum Expense

These provide an instant overview of financial health.

* Expense by Category (Donut Chart)
Shows how expenses are distributed across categories like Rent, Utilities, and Office Supplies.

* Revenue by Category (Donut Chart)
Highlights major revenue sources and their contribution to total income.

* Revenue vs Expense by Department (Bar Chart)
Compares income and spending across departments such as Finance, HR, IT, Marketing, and Operations.

* Monthly Profit Trend (Line Chart)
Displays profit performance over months, helping identify growth or decline patterns.

Interactive Filters (Slicers)
Allows filtering by:

Year

Month

Quarter

Department

* Payment Method
This enables users to analyze financial data from multiple perspectives.

* Navigation Panel (Sidebar Menu)
Includes buttons for Overview, Revenue, Expense, and Profit & Loss sections, improving dashboard usability.

Business Impact & Insights:

* Helps identify departments with high expenses

* Highlights profitable and loss-making periods

* Supports better budget planning

* Improves financial transparency for management

* Reduces dependency on manual reports

This dashboard transforms raw financial data into a structured, decision-support tool.
